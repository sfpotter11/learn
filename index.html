Unicorn of the Sea
Narwhals live in the icy waters of the Arctic seas. Narwhals winter in deep-water beneath ice pack in groups of 5-10 animals; in summer they migrate to shallow, ice-free summer grounds where they congregate in larger groups. They rarely stray far from ice.
